The Ceremony

The reason they chose this venue was that they both shared a University of Chicago bond being alumini. Patrick received his MBA from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. Amber is completing her anasthesia residency this June at the University of Chicago Hospital. Incidentally, within the first 3 months of dating, Patrick broke his hand and required surgury and was treated at this hospital and Amber doctored him back to health. Rockafeller Chapel is located on the Hyde Park campus on the South Side of Chicago.

The Reception

The reception will be held at Cafe Brauer which is located on a pond right outside of the Lincoln Park Zoo. This venue was chosen for its outdoor feel, despite being in the heart of Chicago's Lincoln Park neighborhood. The Lincoln Park Zoo is a free zoo where Amber and Patrick have spent many afternoons walking around and enjoying Chicago's beautiful summer weather.
